Biography

Zhizheng is now a research fellow with Prof. Simon King at the Centre for Speech Technology Research (CSTR), University of Edinburgh, UK. He received the PhD degree from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, under the supervision of Prof. Haizhou LI and Dr. Eng Siong CHNG. He was an intern researcher at Microsoft Research Aisa, China and a visiting researcher at University of Eastern Finland and Tampere University of Technology, Finland. He received the best paper award in Asia Pacific Signal and Information Processing Association Annual Submit and Conference (APSIPA ASC) 2012. He also co-organised the first Automatic Speaker Verification Spoofing and Countermeasures Challenge as a special session in Interspeech 2015.
